Our Story

2020
Nov

Her story…

I met Charles through social networks and the first thing I noticed about him was how he loved going to church and his love for the Lord. We started to communicate via the DM and one of the first major moves he made was to invite me to church.

At first I hesitated but because of the way he carried himself, to how he related with me, I perceived that he was different (in an amazing way) and that's when I decided to take him up on the invite. On the 1st of November 2020 , he came to fetch me from my res in Bellville and we went to his church in Waterfront. Charles was never vague about his intentions. He made it known to me very early on and very loud that he was not one to play games. It was just a few months of knowing each other that he told me that I will be his wife. At first , I was not sold as it had only been a few months but after praying. I had been praying - and then it dawned on my Spirit that - this is it! We went through counseling in preparation for marriage, and today we are excitedly planning our wedding.

2016
Apr

His story…

Kazi, that’s what I call her. I came into the relationship resolute - knowing that in our ministry, we don’t just enter into a relationship to float around. I made the genuineness of my intentions known to her early on, and the direction in which I would like for the relationship to go.

Even when I told her, I was certain that she wasn’t really sure, but my mind was made because the confirmation was by the Holy Ghost. Well the Holy Ghost who’s our extraordinary strategist did the convincing in her heart also and she was on board. Kazi is a special gift from God to me, so easy to talk to, simple to love and gentle to befriend and the best of it is her heart for the Lord and her special love for our Man of God Rev Chris Dsc Dsc DD With the guidance and support of my Highly Esteemed Zonal Pastor, throughout the process, we started counseling. I then proceeded to ask for her parents’ blessings. On the 15th of April 2023, the cows were sent, lobola was paid in full and on the 29th of August 2023 I went on my knees to officially ask her to marry me, she say a resounding YES but I could not remember hearing anything due to the exciting, well her finger was stretched forth and I put the ring. Glorrry. We are grateful to God and our sweet Holy Spirit who has planned everything excellently in due time.

Jan

Our story…

The journey to where we are has been a walk of faith. A friendship marked by intentionality in our actions. A courtship characterised by immense personal & spiritual growth.

A union firmly founded on Christ - the solid rock. Now, we invite our family, friends and colleagues to witness the holy matrimonial celebration. We are grateful for the people God has brought into our lives. Those who have poured into us of themselves, their prayers. To those who have, and continue to cheer us on - we appreciate you! And now these three remain: faith, hope and love. But the greatest of these is love - 1 Cor 13:13 Love so consistent, indescribable, unfathomable. Love unconditional - the love of God.

That is the story of our lives….
